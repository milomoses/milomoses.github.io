As a reward for going on this tour, you get to play my game. Player 1 uses W to accelerate forwards, S to accelerate backwards and A and D to steer. Player 2 is the same but on the arrow keys. Player 1 uses T to shoot and Player 2 uses 1 on the num pad (my apologies to those on computers with no numpads). I reccomend Game Mode 1 for begginers.
